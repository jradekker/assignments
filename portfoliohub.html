My favorite city trips

One of my hobbies is to make city trips.

A few times a year I stay in one of the capitals because of a congress or something like that.

When there is time left I take the opportunity to do some sightseeing.

Walking and biking around in a city is not just my hobby.

My wife is fond of it as well.

Together we've made a lot of pictures.
